# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name
Investment & Expense Tracker

## Version
1.0

## Overview
A personal finance management application that uses Google Sheets as a database and a Progressive Web App (PWA) as the mobile interface. Designed for individuals who want complete ownership of their financial data without paying for subscriptions or sharing data with third-party services.

## Problem Statement
Existing personal finance apps either:
- Require expensive monthly subscriptions (₹499+/month)
- Demand access to SMS, bank accounts, or personal financial data
- Store user data on third-party servers with unclear privacy policies
- Are bloated with features most users never need

Users need a simple, private, free tool to track daily expenses, SIP mutual funds, and stock investments.

## Target User
- Individual investors in India tracking personal finances
- Users comfortable with Google Sheets
- Privacy-conscious users who want data ownership
- Users who want a mobile-first expense tracking experience

## Goals
1. Zero cost to operate (no server, no subscription)
2. Complete data ownership (user's own Google Sheet)
3. Under 5 seconds to add an expense from mobile
4. Secure access (only authorized user can read/write)
5. Works offline with automatic sync
6. Open source and self-hostable
